Our history

White Space is the world's first art museum dedicated entirely to the boundless creativity of children. We believe that true genius lies in the crayons and markers of young artists.

Our collection showcases vibrant, emotional and powerful pieces created by artists aged 4 to 13. Step into a world where technique takes a back seat to emotion, where colours break the rules, and where every canvas offers a glimpse into pure, unfiltered imagination. Visit us and see the world through the eyes of a child again.
